🏠 Did you switch insurance companies after Hurricane Beryl?

Many homeowners don't realize this:

Even if you've changed insurance companies since Hurricane Beryl, the insurance company you had when the storm occurred may still be responsible for covered storm damage. Insurance coverage typically follows the date of loss, not who insures your home today.

If your roof, siding, gutters, or property suffered storm damage from Hurricane Beryl, you may still have options—even if you've already switched carriers. At Aerie Roofing, we help homeowners:

  • ✅ Identify the insurance company that covered the property during the storm
  • ✅ Document and inspect storm-related damage
  • ✅ Review previous claim information
  • ✅ Determine whether the damage came from Beryl, a newer storm, or a combination of both
  • ✅ Understand the next steps available to them

Don't assume you're out of options because you changed insurance companies. A professional inspection and proper documentation can make all the difference.
